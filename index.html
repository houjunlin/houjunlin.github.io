My name is Junlin Hou (侯君临), I am a Post-doc Fellow at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), working with Professor Hao Chen. Before that, I obtained my PhD from the School of Computer Science, Fudan University, supervised by Professor Rui Feng.

My research interest is in explainable aritificial intelligence for medical imaging, at an intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and medical imaging. My recent research topics include:

  • Self-explainable (intrisically interpretable) deep learning
  • Label-efficient deep learning
  • Opthalmology, radiology, dermoscopy
